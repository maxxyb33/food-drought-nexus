About this Website

This interactive website examines historical production and trade of 5 cereal crop products around the world, through various geovisualization techniques.

Data

Discover the crop data from the Food and Agricultural Organization (FAO) of the United Nations.

Purpose

This website allows a visual comparison of historical variations in food production and consumption to droughts, as well as the difference between current and maximum populations that can be supported by the food produced in each country.
